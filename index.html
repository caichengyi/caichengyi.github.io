About Me

Hi there, I am Chengyi Cai, a second year Ph.D. student at Trustworthy Machine Learning and Reasoning Group (TMLR) in the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, the University of Melbourne, luckily advised by Dr. Feng Liu, A/Prof Jianzhong Qi and Dr. Zesheng Ye. I was once supervised by Dr. Yuchen Guo and Prof. Qionghai Dai, and obtained a master's degree from Tsinghua University. My current research interests lie in Learning/Inference under Distribution Shift, including:

  • Model reprogramming of pre-trained vision models and VLMs (Vision-Language Models)
  • Test-time alignment of VLMs and MLLMs (Multimodal Large Language Models)
